If you could “Invent the Future,” how would you do it?

Looking back on the content of my other pages, I found a major theme. I, and many other students, are simply looking for things to do. Specifically, things that will make us better students and career builders. If there was an easy way for students to connect to each other and share ways to “get ahead,” it could be a good way to make students like me achieve things that they otherwise wouldn't have known about.

Yes, we have a career page with job openings. And yes, we have career counseling, tutors, and other such things. But my idea involves peers communicating with each other. And in doing so, we can help each other become “VT-Shaped,” whether we know it or not.

My idea is called “VT Student Counsel.”
