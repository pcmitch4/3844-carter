Inventing the VT-Shaped Student: Student Counsel

Step One: Learn yourself

In order to invent “VT-Shaped” Students, they need to know what that means whether explicitly or implicitly. For me, I implicitly understood that being “VT-Shaped,” or being a well rounded student, involved branching beyond primary interests, finding internships or work outside of the universtiy, and being tech saavy. I didn't have a fancy name for it, like “VT-Shaped,” but the concept makes sense to me now that it has been explicitly drilled in my memory.

So for students that don't have those ideas on their own, my project will first need to establish this.

Step Two: Acknowledge yourself!

So you're already a VT-Shaped Student? Great! Part of being a rounded individual should be to help others be well-rounded too, so a self-sustaining system can be built. Think Ut prosim; that I may serve.

By acknowledging your own strengths and weaknesses as a student, you can serve the community. Use your strengths to build others' weaknesses up, and use others' strengths to build on your own weaknesses. There needs to be a platform for this...

Step Three: The Platform

I propose an online forum, somewhat similar to Reddit.

How will it be the same?

  • User accounts can be anonymous
  • Students can post topics that other students comment on. Comment streams can tangent off of each other.
  • The website should be simple; this is a community of information and content superfluous to the main topic should be filtered out.

How will it be different?

  • The point of this community is to share information to build each other as students and career-seekers. Entertainment and social posts are for someplace else.
  • “Upvoting” and “downvoting” are unnecessary here, as a level community.
