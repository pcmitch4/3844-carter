So What?

If you're reading this page, I sincerely hope that you read the rest of the pages too...

In conclusion, the VT-Shaped initiative is a recruiting tool and a guide to the future of the university. I didn't need to be recruited to this university, I already knew it was where I wanted to study and I would be happy here. Sure, it is important, but I don't hold a lot of stock in the future of the university either.

My individuality and strengths are not defined by Virginia Tech, nor do I want them to be. I love what I've done myself as a student, employee and life-learner. The university can keep doing its thing with office-built initiatives and definitions, but I'm good being separate from that.

Dear reader, I don't have any long-term future plans right now, but I will say this. Be yourself and build yourself based on what you want to do and be. If there is an initative that helps you do this, take part in it, or live by it. If you don't need it, then you don't need it, and that is a-okay.

This is Michael circa today! He is happy that he has spent some time reflecting on the VT-Shaped Student.
