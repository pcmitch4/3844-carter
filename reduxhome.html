Changing Perspectives

It's been a few weeks since I created the VT-Shaped Website. Over the course of ENGL 3844: Writing and Digital Media, I've spent a good deal of time reflecting on the concepts of the course and the previous content I've created. It's time now for a change of pace. You've read about why I thought the VT-Shaped Student initiative wasn't for me, but what does it do for the rest of my community? and does defining the initiative make it more effective? This “Web-Essay Redux” will seek to answer those questions and give my final perspective on the themes of our course.

Rather than revise and change previous work, I've added this new branch to the original site to compare side by side. Information here will be more informed by the course.

Follow the links on the navbar to new perspectives on the VT-Shaped Student and the major projects involved with the second half of our course.
